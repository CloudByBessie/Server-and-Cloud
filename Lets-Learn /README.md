## 🛫 Welcome Aboard

This folder is my **airport‑themed learning hub**, designed to make complex cloud and server concepts intuitive, visual, and memorable.

If you’re new to IT, think of this as your **first day at the airport** — and I’m walking you through the terminals, towers, gates, and systems that keep everything running.

---

## 🛩️ Why an Airport?

Because airports and enterprise environments have something in common:

They are **complex systems** where:

- identities must be verified  
- traffic must be routed  
- resources must be secured  
- operations must be coordinated  
- failures must be understood quickly  

The aviation analogy turns abstract concepts into something you can *see*.

---

## 🛰️ Core Concepts (Airport Style)

Here’s how we translate IT fundamentals into aviation:

- **Identity** → Your boarding pass  
- **Authentication** → TSA checking your ID  
- **Authorization** → Access to specific gates or lounges  
- **DNS** → Air Traffic Control routing planes  
- **Servers** → Terminals hosting passengers  
- **Group Policy** → Airport rules and procedures  
- **Networking** → Runways, taxiways, and communication channels  
- **Security** → TSA, surveillance, restricted zones  

Each lesson in this folder breaks down a topic using this metaphor, then ties it back to real‑world IT.
