### *The origin story behind the battles.*

In the shadows of the digital world, two forces emerged:

**CloudTech** — a disciplined defensive collective built on identity, segmentation, and precision.  
A fortress of logic, structure, and resilience.

**Thunderbyte** — a rogue adversary born from chaos, misconfigurations, and the forgotten corners of enterprise networks.  
It thrives where systems drift, where assumptions go unchecked, and where complexity becomes vulnerability.

Their conflict is inevitable.

CloudTech responds — analyzing, isolating, and restoring order with methodical engineering.

This universe is more than storytelling.

- **Cinematic** — a narrative that makes the scenario memorable

- **Recruiter‑friendly** — clear impact, clear skills, clear outcomes
